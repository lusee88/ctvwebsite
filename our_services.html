LIMs (Laboratory Information Systems) Validation

With LIMs being such a crucial part of dealing with data in a clinical labratory, its validation must be handled with great detail and efficiency. The data that is managed, stored, and produced must be reliable and compliant to industry standards.

Validation Plans (VP)

A Validation Plan is a document which provides a high level overview of the risk based validation approach prior to any intial validation activities. The validation deliverables (documents/SOPs) will be defined and risk reduction strategies and testing are outlined. The project level validation plan should not be confused with the Master Validation Plan.

Enterprise Resource Planning Systems (ERP's)

Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) Systems are software that integrates various essential business processes into one fully functioning system to streamline the information throughout the corporation.

GXP Risk Assessment

GxP determinations and any following Risk Assessments are performed on all processes, equipment, and computerized systems which may either directly or indirectly impact the product/patient.

Qualifications

Equipment Qualifications

CVT serves to ensure our clients are supplied with the most efficient and effective systems and equipment. Our immense range of experiences span from the qualification of individual pieces of equipment to entire facilies. Our highly specilized employees perform a consistent systematic approach that is flexible enough to adjust to each company's specific needs.

Installation Qualification

Installation qualification's purpose is to ensure that the quality of installations (such as machines, measuring devices, utilities andmanufacturing areas) used in a manufacturing process are appropriately selected, correctly installed, and operate in accordance with established specifications.

Operational Qualification

A well documented protocol to guarantee the systems or subsystems perform as intended over all anticipated operating ranges through a series of test cases.

Performance Qualification

A well documented protocol to verify that the equipment or system operates consistently. The protocol ensures reproductivity within defined specifications and parameters for prolonged periods during execution of business case senarios.
