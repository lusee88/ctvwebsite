Our Mission

Critical Validation Technologies (CVT) is an amalgamation of industry experts who have elected to work in synergy to bring the best in calibration and validation services to the pharmaceutical, biopharmaceutical, nutraceutical and allied industries.  We are proficient through industry experience and get the job done right, the first time every time.  CVT is reshaping how North America companies implement and qualify equipment and processes. Our strategic focus is sharpened by what we do best: Calibration and Qualification. Whether it's a small project or a challenging facility wide initiative we have the expertise.

Our Values

Even though the North American business landscape may be changing, our commitment to quality has not. We conduct our business ethically and responsibly. The compliance and satisfaction of clients is paramount to CVT and is the core principal upon which the company is founded.

Our Team

Our people are the foundation of our success. CVT has greater than 100 years of combined calibration and validation experience. Our staff utilize their experiences to decrease costs, increase output and ensure quality. With the industry and regulatory expertise our employees share means that the job gets done on time, under budget, and in a compliant manner.
